Our MR & VR Course DNA
With hand-picked instructors, a focus on networking with senior-level industry professionals, and 1-on-1 mentoring, we support our students with world-class content and personal advice, helping you to choose the best next step in your metaverse career.

Live Group Sessions
There's nothing better than live mentorship. We're meeting 2-3 times each week to do code reviews. Our instructors are online 24/7 to unblock you fast whenever you need support with your VR Dev project.

Hand-Picked Instructors
All XR Bootcamp Instructors are top successful VR professionals passionate about sharing their expertise. We are only hiring top trainers that have a proven track record at triple AAA studios or have launched successful VR apps.

1-on-1 Mentoring
Sometimes, it just feels better to connect with an expert privately. Ask for a 1-on-1 session with our advanced-level trainers or XR career advisors.  We’re here to help you succeed!

Close-Knit Community
We highly value and empower personal connections. Benefit from a super active network of students, alumni, and experts sharing opportunities, advice, and discussions on the latest XR tech release.

Project-Based Learning
All our courses focus on enabling you to become a self-sufficient VR developer. At graduation, you will be proud to look at your achievements, learnings, and impressive VR project portfolio. 

Think like a Pro VR Dev
Evolve into a quick-thinking hacker, and learn the secrets of rapid prototyping and VR development from the best of the best VR instructors. Fail fast, learn fast, and elevate your spatial computing mindset.
