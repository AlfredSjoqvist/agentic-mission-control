FRUSTRAIN: TRAINMAN has been selected for the XR Experiences Competition at SXSW 2026.
The project has followed a long development path, including pitch selections at Festival international du film d'animation d'Annecy MIFA, NewImages Festival, and participation in the European Creators’ Lab (EUCL). 
Demo of the TRAINMAN chapter of FRUSTRAIN was supported by VIVERSE through the Creators Program - it is available as WebXR/Mobile/Desktop experience on VIVERSE and our web-site. It is already has over 26K visits.

At SXSW 2026, FRUSTRAIN: TRAINMAN will have its world premiere in its full version, presented as part of the official XR Experiences Competition lineup.

We are really grateful to all our team members and partners, who helped and are helping us along the way. 
Main financing of this project - years of passion and personal dedication of the creator, Pavel Pavlyukov, contributions from my Film XR.
VIVERSE / Michael Morran helped us to shape the project from story prototype to real thing. 
Currently, the project is co-developed with Polish Feeling Digital (Mitya Sorkin, Pavel Lukashevich) team and Serbian Pixels2Pixels Studio (Djordje Djordjic).


FRUSTRAIN is a first-person anti-utopian narrative adventure set aboard a surreal Eastern European night train. Through environmental storytelling, psychological horror, and puzzle-driven exploration, the experience follows a train conductor trapped in his carriage for decades, reliving a single crime he once chose to ignore.
 
The project unfolds like a fragmented dream — looping, unstable, and deeply personal. It reflects lots of internal fears we all are facing, trying to run away from inevitable evil. 

We are pretty sure, that the project would grow into something bigger as we are heading for a store release in Q3/Q4 2026 with TRAINMAN, as well as planning to expand to a bigger FRUSTRAIN Game release later.

Thanks, SXSW, Blake Kammerdiener for trusting us, our train is departing and is headed to Austin, USA.
